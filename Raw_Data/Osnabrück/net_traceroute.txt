Dataset format:
site_name: str; represents measurement site
timestamp_start: ISO8601
timestamp_end: ISO8601
hop: int
target: str
ip_protocol: str
hop_id: str
asn: str
packet_loss: float; in %
packets_send: int
ping_avg: float; in ms
ping_worst: float; in ms
ping_best: float; in ms
ping_stddev: float; in ms
