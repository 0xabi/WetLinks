Dataset format:
site_name: str; represents measurement site
timestamp: ISO8601
state: str
uplink: float; in Mbit/s
downlink: float; in Mbit/s
pop_ping_latency: float; in ms
ping_drop: float; in %
mean_ping_latency: float; in ms
ping_stdvar: float; in ms
fraction_obstructed: float; in %
obstruction_duration: float; in s
obstruction_interval: float; in s
direction_azimuth: float; in °
direction_elevation: float; in °
